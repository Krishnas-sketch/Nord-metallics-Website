A critical input for the future of steel

Direct Reduced Iron is a high-quality metallic input used in Electric Arc Furnace steelmaking. Produced through a controlled reduction process, DRI supports metallics balance, steelmaking consistency and greater flexibility in modern furnace operations.

As steel producers continue to modernize and prioritize higher-quality, lower-emission inputs, DRI is expected to play an increasingly important role in the metallics supply chain.
