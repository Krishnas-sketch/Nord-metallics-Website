About

A platform built for the future of steel.

Nord Metallics is an industrial development platform focused on strategically positioned metallics projects that support modern steelmaking, strengthen supply chain resilience and align with the long-term evolution of the US steel industry.

Built on industrial discipline, technical credibility and long-term strategic vision, the company is being developed to serve the evolving needs of Electric Arc Furnace steelmakers.

Company Overview

Nord Metallics is focused on advancing large-scale metallics infrastructure that supports cleaner steel production, stronger supply chains and long-term industrial resilience.

With a clear focus on Direct Reduced Iron and related metallics solutions, the company is positioning itself to serve the evolving needs of Electric Arc Furnace steelmakers. Backed by leadership with deep experience across major industrial developments, project finance, engineering coordination, regulatory engagement and execution planning, Nord Metallics is building with discipline, clarity and long-term intent.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To develop strategically positioned, future-ready metallics platforms that support cleaner steelmaking, stronger supply chains and long-term industrial value creation.

Vision: To become a trusted developer of next-generation low-carbon metallics infrastructure through industrial vision, technical credibility, disciplined execution and strategic partnerships.
