Low-Carbon Metallics

The future of the U.S. steel industry will increasingly depend on access to dependable, high-quality, lower-carbon metallics inputs. As Electric Arc Furnace capacity continues to grow and steel producers place greater emphasis on feedstock quality, domestic supply resilience and lower-emission production pathways, low-carbon metallics are becoming a critical enabler of next-generation steelmaking.

Nord Metallics is developing projects designed to support this transition by aligning with the needs of modern U.S. steel producers, strengthening the domestic metallics supply chain and contributing to the long-term evolution of a more competitive and lower-carbon steel industry.

We believe low-carbon metallics represent not a niche opportunity, but a strategically important part of the future industrial foundation of U.S. steelmaking.

Serving the EAF Industry

Electric Arc Furnace steelmaking is becoming increasingly important to the long-term competitiveness of the U.S. steel sector. With EAF producers continuing to expand capacity and place greater emphasis on feedstock quality, operating flexibility and lower-carbon production pathways, the need for reliable, high-quality metallics is growing in strategic importance.

Nord Metallics is being developed to support these market requirements through projects focused on quality, infrastructure advantage and long-term supply relevance for the evolving U.S. EAF industry.

Strengthening Supply Chain Resilience

A more resilient metallics supply chain is becoming critical to the long-term competitiveness of the U.S. steel industry. Steel producers increasingly require greater confidence in raw material quality, supply availability and logistics reliability as they modernize operations and respond to changing market conditions.

Nord Metallics is being developed with this need in mind. Our strategic focus includes advancing metallics platforms that can help strengthen domestic supply security, improve supply chain positioning and support the next generation of U.S. steel production.

Strategic Partnerships

The successful development of major industrial projects requires more than capital and technical ambition. It requires the right partnership framework across technology, engineering, project execution, industrial infrastructure, supply chain support and financing.

Nord Metallics is being built with this principle at its core. We believe high-quality partnerships are fundamental to developing projects that are credible, executable and positioned to create durable value in the evolving U.S. steel sector.

Scalable Platform Building

Nord Metallics is not being developed as a one-project story. We are building a scalable industrial platform designed to grow over time, expand strategically and remain relevant as the U.S. steel industry continues to evolve.

Our approach is centered on creating long-term value through projects that are not only commercially and technically sound today, but also capable of supporting future growth, broader market participation and a stronger position within the domestic metallics supply chain. We believe this platform-oriented model is fundamental to building a durable business with lasting industrial and investor relevance.
