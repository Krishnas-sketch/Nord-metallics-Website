Leadership Overview

Our leadership team reflects the multidisciplinary nature of industrial project development. Nord Metallics is being built through a combination of technical depth, project execution capability, commercial strategy, government engagement and long-term business vision.

Executive Leadership

Bijoy Nair

Co-Founder and Chief Commercial Officer

Bijoy Nair brings more than three decades of experience across new business development, corporate finance, project structuring, strategic partnerships, government relations and cross-border investment initiatives.

At Nord Metallics, he leads the company’s commercial direction, strategic stakeholder engagement, partnerships and broader business development activities.

Samuel Varghese

Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer

Samuel Varghese brings deep technical and operational experience in Direct Reduced Iron and related industrial processes, with decades of involvement across plant development, engineering, technology alignment, execution and commissioning.

He leads the company’s technical strategy, project development inputs and technology-related direction.

Sohan Miyawala

Senior Vice President - Projects & Engineering

Sohan Miyawala brings substantial experience in industrial project development and engineering execution. His background supports Nord Metallics’ focus on disciplined project planning, engineering integration, cost awareness and execution readiness.

He plays a key role in project coordination, engineering oversight and development planning.

Raj Dash

Vice President - Governmental, External & Regulatory Affairs

Raj Dash supports Nord Metallics across regulatory coordination, government relations, external stakeholder engagement and institutional interface.

He plays an important role in building and maintaining the external relationships that support long-term project advancement.

Management Philosophy

Nord Metallics is guided by a leadership philosophy centered on strategic vision, disciplined execution, partnership orientation and long-term value creation.
