Strategically positioned metallics projects

Nord Metallics is advancing metallics projects designed to support modern steelmaking through high-quality inputs, infrastructure strength and long-term industrial relevance.

The company is being developed as an industrial platform, not as a collection of stand-alone opportunities. Each project is evaluated through a disciplined framework that considers market relevance, infrastructure access, utility availability, technical viability, capital efficiency, environmental readiness and long-term scalability.

Featured Project

Louisiana DRI Development

Our current flagship development is a planned low-carbon Direct Reduced Iron project in Louisiana, strategically positioned to serve the North American steel industry.

The project reflects Nord Metallics' approach to industrial development: relevant market demand, strong logistics connectivity, industrial utility access and a platform capable of long-term growth.
