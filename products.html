Metallics solutions for modern steelmaking

Nord Metallics is focused on Direct Reduced Iron and related metallics solutions that can support Electric Arc Furnace steelmakers with reliable quality, stronger metallics balance and alignment with lower-carbon industrial pathways.

Product development is being shaped around the needs of modern steel producers: consistency, supply security, operational flexibility and long-term relevance in a changing steelmaking landscape.

Direct Reduced Iron

A high-quality metallic input used in Electric Arc Furnace steelmaking to support consistency, quality and greater control over metallics balance.

Related Metallics

Future-ready metallics pathways intended to strengthen supply reliability and complement the evolving needs of regional steel producers.

Supply Chain Relevance

Strategically located metallics production can enhance supply security, reduce logistics complexity and support regional industrial competitiveness.

How DRI Supports the Steel Value Chain

  • Improved raw material consistency
  • Better metallics supply security
  • Stronger support for Electric Arc Furnace steelmaking
  • Alignment with lower-carbon industrial pathways
  • Long-term resilience in regional steel supply chains
